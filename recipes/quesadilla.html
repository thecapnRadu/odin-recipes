Chicken Quesadilla

Description

Let's be honest, who doesn't love quesadillas? They are juicy, easy and fast to make, and they are perfect for almost any occasion, especially for those weekend days spent with the friends!

Without further ado, I will show you the easiest recipe for a delicious chicken quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams Cheddar Cheese
  • 400 grams Chicken Breast
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 4 Tortillas
  • Salt
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • Sunflower Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a medium frying pan with oil over medium heat until it's hot
  2. Wash and cut the breast chicken into small pieces, and add the chicken seasoning
  3. Cook the chicken for about 5 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, cut the tomato, red onion, and bell pepper into small pieces, and add salt
  5. When the chicken is almost cooked, add the tomato, red onion, and bell pepper in the pan and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat until hot, about 3 minutes. Place a tortilla in the pan and sprinkle with half of the chicken mixture, and half of the cheddar cheese. Top with a second tortilla and cook until the underside of the bottom tortilla is golden brown in several spots and half of the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes. Using a spatula, carefully flip the quesadilla over and cook until the underside of the second tortilla is crisp and golden brown in several spots and all of the cheese is melted, about 2 to 3 minutes more.
  7. Slide the quesadilla from the pan onto a cutting board and cut into wedges. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make a second quesadilla. Serve topped with guacamole, salsa, and sour cream, if desired.
