Chilli Con Carne

Description

This hearty, all-time classic chilli con carne recipe is hard to beat. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Minced Beef
  • 400 grams Tin of Red Kidney Beans
  • 800 grams Tin of Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 Medium Carrots
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Medium Onions
  • 2 Red Peppers
  • 1 teaspoon Chilli Powder
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the onions, garlic, and carrots. Halve the red peppers, remove the stalks and seeds and roughly chop.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large casserole pan on a medium-high heat, add the chopped vegetables, chilli powder, and a good pinch of sea salt and black pepper, then cook for 7 minutes, or until softened, stirring regularly.
  3. Drain and add the kidney beans, tip in the tomatoes, breaking them up with the back of a spoon, then pour in 1 tin's worth of water. Add the minced beef, breaking any larger chunks.
  4. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer with a lid slightly for 1 hour, or until slightly thickened and reduced, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve up with fluffy rice or couscous, a hunk of crusty bread, or over a jacket potato, with some yoghurt, guacamole, and wedges of lime on the side for squeezing over.
